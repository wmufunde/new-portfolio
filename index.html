I am a web developer from Croydon, London. I have a passion for the creative, with eclectic taste for development and design. My primary language is Python, though I also have interests in Ruby and Java

I got my first taste of programming studying Computer Science at Kent University, and fell in love with web development. I was taught the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, Javascript, and PHP, and discovered a new world of possibilities. Unfortunatley I was unable to finish the course due to circumstances out of my control. But I was determiened to be a programmer, and so I found another route to my goal.

Unwilling to give up, I decided to embark on a self funded course that fit around my schedule. I am currently undertaking the Development Professional Programming course with Just IT in London
